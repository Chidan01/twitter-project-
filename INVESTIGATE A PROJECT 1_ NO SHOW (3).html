INTRODUCTION¶

The data set gives an insight of an hospital having patient who do not show up for their appointment day, and would try in predicting the which of the factors are the likely cause of this. So i would be using pandas, numpy and matplotlib, to try and predict which feature has good correlation with the target variable. The data specifcally is about how an hospital ssems to negivate a problem why some of their patient miss/ do not show up on their appoinment day. Below is a brief decription of what the dataset looks like;

PatientId -Identification of a patient¶

AppointmentID- Identification of each appointment¶

Gender - Male or Female¶

ScheduledDay - The day of the actuall appointment, when they have to visit the doctor.¶

AppointmentDay - The day someone called or registered the appointment, this is before appointment of course.¶

Age - How old is the patient.¶

Neighbourhood - Where the appointment takes place.¶

Scholarship - True of False¶

Hipertension - True of False¶

Diabetes - True of False¶

Alcoholism - True of False¶

Handcap - True of False¶

SMS_received - 1 or more messages sent to the patient¶

No-show - True of False¶

Questions that i found useful in getting insight, and i can explore.¶

Now i am going to the check how, why, the independent variables relates with the depent variable (No show). Below are some set of questions to be used to answer the question;

1. What percentage of patient show up and didn't?¶

2.Which of the Gender misses their appiontment day or did not show up¶

3.How many of the patient took the scholarship and show up, and What percentage of patient who took the scholarship and did not?¶

4.how many patient received sms and attended their appointment?¶

5. Is SMS_received and gender affect the patient's show up?¶

6 Is any deseases (Hipertension , Diabetes ) affect the patient's show up?¶
